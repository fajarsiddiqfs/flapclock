About: Flap Clock – For Desktop & Mobile

Flap Clock was built with a simple idea: time should feel effortless. In a world full of distractions, most clocks try to do too much—or not enough. Flap Clock strips it back to what matters: clarity, precision, and presence.

A fullscreen flip clock that feels immediate, beautiful, and invisible at the same time. It's designed for any device, any moment—desktop or mobile—so the time is always exactly where you need it, when you need it. Nothing more. Nothing less.
